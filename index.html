Our Story

Who We Are

Robinson Roots is a family-founded 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in rural Arizona. We are first-time minority ranchers working from the ground up to build a legacy of knowledge, opportunity, and abundance.

We're here to learn, grow, and share our journey with others — especially those who have been underserved and overlooked. Our property serves as a working ranch and educational space where visitors can reconnect with nature, learn life skills, and experience healing through agriculture and animals.

We are building something meaningful — brick by brick, seed by seed, relationship by relationship. Our journey is real, our roots run deep, and our mission is bigger than us.

We grow. We learn. We share. Together, we thrive.

Our Mission

Rooted in Purpose.
Growing Generations.

"Robinson Roots is dedicated to educating and empowering individuals — especially youth — through hands-on experiences in agriculture, including gardening, animal care, sustainable farming, and food production. Our mission is to cultivate life skills, self-sufficiency, and generational knowledge by connecting people to the land, teaching them how to grow."

Programs & Experiences

Transformative, hands-on programs connecting people to the land, to animals, and to each other through agriculture and nature.

Agricultural Education

Teaching practical skills for lifelong growth and self-reliance through real farm experience.

  • Gardening workshops & food growing
  • Hydroponics & sustainable farming
  • Food production & preservation
  • Seed-to-table education
🐾

Animal Care & Husbandry

Responsible care and sustainable practices through meaningful animal interaction.

  • Pig education & care
  • Bunny interaction area
  • Chicken & egg education
  • Animal care learning
☮️

Therapeutic Outdoor Experiences

A serenity space for healing, connection, and peace through nature.

  • Serenity garden & quiet spaces
  • Nature therapy sessions
  • Outdoor family time
  • Wellness & mindfulness experiences

Youth & School Programs

Creating opportunities and inspiring the next generation of leaders and land stewards.

  • Church camps & youth days
  • Homeschool agricultural visits
  • Youth mentorship programs

  • Family gardening opportunities
